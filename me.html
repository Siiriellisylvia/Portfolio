Who am I?

Curious, open-minded, reliable

I pride myself on my "I'll find out"-skills

After spending the last 10 years living and working in 3 different countries around Europe, I've now found my home in Denmark.

I'm 32, and would call myself a lifetime learner. I already have a degree in clothing design and business, and I enjoy developing new skills. I have tried figure skating, boxing and wakeboarding among others - with mixed results.

After trying many things, I have found my childhood passion again in frontend development. I have basic knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and I'm looking forward to learning React and Firebase this semester.

During my years abroad, I've gained experience in working in multicultural environments, communication accross teams and in many different areas.

Creativity and helping others are a central part of my life - I'm the happiest when I can see the results of my work and the positive impact it can have. be it a script that finally cooperates, helping a colleague to find a good solution to a problem, or figuring out how to knit clothes for the new baby in the family. As cheesy as it sounds - it can be anything from helping a friend, a team member, or volunteering with animals (though I would probably pay money to hang out with kittens).
