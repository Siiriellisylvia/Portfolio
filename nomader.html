Nomader

Landing page for a travel app

September 2023

1 week

Group of 4

ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly, Looka, Figma

This project was a short demonstration of the power of AI in design and development

Our group designed the solution for an AI generated target group of Budget Explorer Nomads. We asked ChatGPT to come up with colours, typography and the features of the app, generated illustrations in Adobe firefly and logo with Looka. Based on this, we created the prototype in Figma and generated all content text using ChatGPT as well.
